# Issue Map

Use this map to ensure full business-law coverage during issue spotting.

## Table of contents

1. Corporate and governance
2. Contracts and commercial terms
3. Employment and contractors
4. Intellectual property
5. Privacy and data handling
6. Marketing and consumer protection
7. Regulatory and licensing
8. Disputes and enforcement

## 1) Corporate and governance

Check:
- Entity formation and state registration
- Board/member approvals for major actions
- Cap table, equity issuance, and transfer controls
- Inter-company agreements and conflicts

## 2) Contracts and commercial terms

Check:
- Scope/deliverables acceptance definitions
- Payment terms, late fees, refund rights
- Limitation of liability and indemnity
- Termination, renewal, assignment, and dispute clauses

## 3) Employment and contractors

Check:
- Worker classification (employee vs contractor)
- Offer letters and restrictive covenants
- Wage/hour and leave obligations
- Termination process and documentation

## 4) Intellectual property

Check:
- Ownership chain for code/content/brand assets
- Work-made-for-hire and assignment clauses
- Trademark use and clearance signals
- Open-source license obligations

## 5) Privacy and data handling

Check:
- Data collected and lawful basis
- Privacy notice alignment with practice
- Vendor/data processor obligations
- Security controls and incident response path

## 6) Marketing and consumer protection

Check:
- Advertising claims substantiation
- Required disclosures (affiliate/sponsored/fees)
- Subscription auto-renew compliance
- Refund/cancellation policy clarity

## 7) Regulatory and licensing

Check:
- Industry-specific permits or licenses
- Cross-border restrictions
- Financial/service-specific compliance duties
- Recordkeeping and reporting obligations

## 8) Disputes and enforcement

Check:
- Existing claims, notices, or chargebacks
- Litigation hold or evidence preservation needs
- Arbitration/forum clauses
- Settlement posture and deadlines
