# Compliance Checklist

Project: value-ladder-production
Scope: all funnel pages, emails, offers, and paid promotion assets.

## 1) Required Legal Pages

Must exist and be linked in site footer and checkout:
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Earnings Disclaimer
- Affiliate Disclosure
- Refund Policy
- Contact page with business entity details

Action:
- Publish legal pages before paid traffic starts.

## 2) FTC Disclosure Requirements

Where disclosures are mandatory:
- Any affiliate link in emails, pages, or downloadable assets
- Any sponsored placement or paid endorsement
- Any testimonial with material connection

Disclosure standards:
- Clear, conspicuous, plain language
- Place near CTA/affiliate links (not hidden in footer)
- Repeat disclosure in email body above first affiliate link

Approved baseline copy:
- "Disclosure: I may earn a commission if you buy through links on this page, at no extra cost to you."

## 3) Offer and Earnings Claims

Rules:
- Avoid guaranteed income claims
- Avoid unverifiable superlatives ("best", "guaranteed") without evidence
- Include results disclaimer where outcomes are shown

Required disclaimer on sales pages/webinars:
- "Results vary based on effort, market, and execution. No specific outcomes are guaranteed."

## 4) Email Compliance

Checklist:
- Include sender identity and physical mailing address
- Include one-click unsubscribe in every campaign email
- Honor unsubscribe within required legal timeframe
- Do not use deceptive subject lines

## 5) Data and Consent

Checklist:
- Consent language on all opt-in forms
- Cookie notice and consent management where required
- Data retention policy documented
- DPA/processors list for email and analytics tools

## 6) Copyright and Asset Licensing

Checklist:
- Keep license records for stock images/audio/fonts
- Track source URLs + license terms in asset register
- Do not use unlicensed user-generated content in ads

## 7) High-Ticket Sales and Contracts

Checklist:
- Signed service agreement required pre-onboarding
- Scope, payment terms, and refund terms clearly stated
- Usage rights and confidentiality clauses included

## 8) Funnel-Level QA Gates

Before publish:
- Compliance review for each landing page and email sequence
- Verify disclosure placement above fold where relevant
- Verify checkout terms acceptance checkbox
- Verify privacy-policy link on all forms
