# High-Ticket Offer Terms

Offer: Elite Advisory ($12,000)
Purpose: formalize scope, compensation, usage boundaries, and qualification standards.

## 1) Scope of Work

Duration:
- 12 weeks

Deliverables:
- Bi-weekly 1:1 strategy calls (6 total)
- Weekly async decision support (Mon-Fri)
- Custom growth roadmap and KPI scorecard
- Funnel audits with action plans

Client obligations:
- Provide implementation updates weekly
- Maintain baseline tracking events and dashboards
- Execute agreed sprint priorities

## 2) Compensation Model

Primary model:
- Flat fee: $12,000

Payment terms:
- Option A: one payment ($12,000 upfront)
- Option B: 3 payments of $4,300 (30-day cadence)

Performance add-on (optional):
- 5% revenue-share on net new revenue above agreed baseline for 90 days

## 3) Qualification Criteria

Must-have:
- Existing offer with proof of market demand
- Revenue baseline of at least $5,000/month
- Capacity to execute weekly sprint actions
- Access to funnel and analytics stack

Disqualifiers:
- No offer-market fit evidence
- No implementation bandwidth
- Purely informational engagement with no execution intent

## 4) Content Usage Rights

By default:
- Client retains full ownership of business assets and content
- Advisor may reference anonymized outcomes unless client opts out in writing

Brand/testimonial rights:
- Any named testimonial or logo usage requires written consent
- No paid media usage of client brand/content without explicit permission

## 5) Exclusivity Clause

Mutual category exclusivity:
- During engagement and 30 days post-engagement, advisor will not accept direct competitors in the same narrow niche/geography without client consent.

Client-side exclusivity:
- Client agrees not to engage another primary funnel strategy advisor during active term without prior written disclosure.

## 6) Renewal and Exit

Renewal:
- Month-to-month advisory retainer available after week 12 at $3,500/month

Early exit:
- No refunds on completed sessions/work periods
- Remaining payment obligations survive unless material breach is proven
