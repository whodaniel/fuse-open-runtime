# Niche Skill Seeds

Use these candidate skill ideas when the plan reveals domain gaps that are repeatedly blocking progress.

## Table of contents

1. Market intelligence
2. Pricing and monetization
3. Sales and partnerships
4. Operations and delivery
5. Finance and risk

## 1) Market intelligence

- `tam-sam-som-modeler`
  - Trigger: Need transparent market sizing with assumptions and sensitivity.
- `voice-of-customer-researcher`
  - Trigger: Need structured customer interviews and synthesis.
- `category-positioning-strategist`
  - Trigger: Need defensible positioning against substitutes.

## 2) Pricing and monetization

- `pricing-experiment-designer`
  - Trigger: Need pricing tests and value-based packaging decisions.
- `recurring-revenue-architect`
  - Trigger: Need subscription, retention, and expansion logic.
- `gross-margin-optimizer`
  - Trigger: Need margin improvement playbooks per offer.

## 3) Sales and partnerships

- `enterprise-deal-qualification-agent`
  - Trigger: Need repeatable B2B opportunity qualification.
- `channel-partnership-builder`
  - Trigger: Need partner-sourced growth model design.
- `pipeline-forecast-operator`
  - Trigger: Need forecast reliability and stage conversion controls.

## 4) Operations and delivery

- `capacity-planning-operator`
  - Trigger: Need staffing and delivery capacity model tied to growth.
- `service-delivery-quality-controller`
  - Trigger: Need standardized QA and delivery playbooks.
- `automation-opportunity-mapper`
  - Trigger: Need process automation candidates ranked by ROI.

## 5) Finance and risk

- `cashflow-scenario-analyst`
  - Trigger: Need monthly cash visibility across scenarios.
- `capital-strategy-advisor`
  - Trigger: Need bootstrapping vs debt vs equity pathway analysis.
- `risk-early-warning-builder`
  - Trigger: Need leading indicators and trigger thresholds for strategic risk.

## Selection rule

Promote a seed into a real skill when:

1. The gap blocks a current milestone.
2. The same gap appears in two or more planning cycles.
3. The skill would likely be reusable across multiple plans.
