[Privacy Policy for The New Fuse](https://thenewfuse.com/privacy-policy)

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

### 1. Introduction

The New Fuse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about
you when you use TNF extension services.

### 2. Information We Collect

- **Authentication Data**: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email
  address and basic profile information to create your account.
- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with the
  extension, such as the number of videos processed and feature usage, for
  analytics purposes.
- **Content Data**: We process the video URLs and transcripts you submit to
  provide the AI analysis service. We do not store video content permanently on
  our servers unless explicitly saved to your library.

### 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- To process billing and subscriptions via TNF platform billing services.
- To communicate with you about updates and support.

### 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

- **Service Providers**: Google (for AI processing) and TNF platform
  infrastructure/services.
- **Legal Compliance**: If required by law.

### 5. Your Rights

You may request to delete your account and data at any time by contacting
support@thenewfuse.com.

### 6. Contact Us

For any questions, please contact us at support@thenewfuse.com.
