## Summary

Jordan 'Poopy' Castro, co-founder of Doodles, discusses his journey from early
crypto prediction markets and CryptoKitties to creating one of the most
successful NFT brands. The conversation explores strategic community management,
the technical history of NFT genetics, and the transition from decentralized
experiments to building mainstream intellectual property.

## Key Points

- Jordan's background at Dapper Labs and leadership of the CryptoKitties project
  provided the operational blueprint for Doodles.
- The Doodles team intentionally gated their Discord at 1,100 members to
  cultivate a dedicated core of advocates rather than a speculative crowd.
- Project success is attributed to a 'three-pronged stool' of universally
  inclusive art, community-led governance via the Doodlebank, and an experienced
  team.
- The founders chose to avoid early 'rarity tools' to encourage collectors to
  appreciate the art rather than just speculative metrics.
- Doodles aims to act as a 'Trojan horse' for mass blockchain adoption by
  focusing on brand building and consumer-facing IP.
