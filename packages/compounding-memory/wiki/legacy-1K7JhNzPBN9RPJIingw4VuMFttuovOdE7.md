## Summary

This video showcases a fully autonomous AI agent workflow designed to convert
TechCrunch news articles into complete, published YouTube videos. The pipeline
utilizes a combination of specialized models like Gemini 2.0 Flash, DeepSeek R1,
and OpenAI's o3-mini to handle everything from news scraping and scriptwriting
to video editing and uploading.

## Key Points

- Development of an end-to-end autonomous video production pipeline.
- Strategic model selection: Gemini for scraping/metadata, DeepSeek R1 for
  reasoning/scripting, and o3-mini for video assembly.
- The importance of using SRT files generated by Whisper to precisely align
  video clips with voiceover timing.
- Use of Regex to clean AI-generated scripts by removing reasoning tokens and
  asterisks before sending to Eleven Labs.
- Demonstration of the system's ability to select contextually relevant visual
  clips based on script topics.
- Automated YouTube publishing including thumbnail selection, title generation,
  and description writing via the Google API.
