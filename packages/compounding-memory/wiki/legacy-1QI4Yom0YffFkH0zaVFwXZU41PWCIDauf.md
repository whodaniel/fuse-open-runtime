## Summary

This video covers a major week in AI releases, highlighting new tools for 4x
video upscaling, consistent manga colorization, and ultra-fast text-to-speech
models. It also details massive updates like MiniMax-1's 4-million-token context

## Key Points

- Star is a new open-source AI capable of 4x video upscaling with high temporal
  consistency.
- Manga Ninja allows for consistent colorization of comic art using reference
  images and point-driven control.
- Kokuro TTS is a highly efficient 82M parameter model ranked #1 on
  text-to-speech leaderboards.
- SVFR (Stable Video Face Restoration) provides a unified system for face
  sharpening, colorization, and inpainting.
- ChatGPT 'Tasks' allows users to schedule recurring prompts or one-time
  reminders within the interface.
- MiniMax-1 released a model with an industry-leading 4-million-token context
  window.
- Nvidia's Sauna image generator now supports 4K resolution at high speeds under
  an Apache 2.0 license.
- Luma Labs launched Ray 2, a significant upgrade in cinematic quality and
  motion consistency for AI video.
- Unitree demonstrated a humanoid robot capable of natural running and jogging
  on uneven terrain.
