## Summary

This video provides a comprehensive guide on using ChatGPT to engineer
high-quality cinematic prompts for AI video generators like Runway and MiniMax.
It introduces a specific framework focusing on the subject, scene, and camera
movement to transform simple text descriptions into professional-grade video
content.

## Key Points

- Effective video prompts must define the medium, subject, motion, scene
  environment, and specific camera settings.
- Using ChatGPT as a 'Prompt Engineer' allows users to automate the creation of
  detailed, cinemagraphic language.
- The 'Subject-Scene-Camera' framework is the core pillar for generating
  realistic AI video.
- Image-to-Video techniques generally provide more control and higher quality
  than Text-to-Video by establishing visual context first.
- Unified aesthetic prompts can be used to maintain visual consistency across
  multiple shots for filmmaking.
- Advanced workflows involve using ChatGPT to generate both a Midjourney image
  prompt and a Runway video prompt simultaneously for maximum consistency.
