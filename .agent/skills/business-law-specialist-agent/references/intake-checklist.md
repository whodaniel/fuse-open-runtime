# Intake Checklist

Gather these inputs before legal-risk analysis. Ask only what is needed for the current decision.

## Table of contents

1. Core context
2. Jurisdiction and governing law
3. Business structure
4. Event or decision details
5. Supporting records
6. Urgency and risk appetite

## 1) Core context

- What decision or event needs legal review?
- What outcome does the user want?
- What actions have already been taken?

## 2) Jurisdiction and governing law

- Country and state/province
- Contract governing law clause (if applicable)
- Where customers/employees/vendors are located

If missing, mark analysis as preliminary and request jurisdiction details.

## 3) Business structure

- Entity type (LLC, C-Corp, sole proprietorship, partnership, etc.)
- Owners and control structure
- Operating agreements/bylaws/shareholder agreements status

## 4) Event or decision details

- Deal, contract, hiring/firing, IP usage, policy launch, dispute, or regulatory notice
- Counterparties involved
- Amount at stake and potential downside
- Key dates and deadlines

## 5) Supporting records

- Contracts and amendments
- Policies/terms/privacy notices
- Communications and evidence trail
- Invoices, payment terms, and performance records

## 6) Urgency and risk appetite

- Hard deadline for decision
- Tolerance for legal uncertainty (`low`, `medium`, `high`)
- Need for immediate outside counsel
