# Question Bank

Use this bank to run focused discovery rounds. Ask at most 3 questions per round and prioritize unanswered high-impact areas first.

## Table of contents

1. Planning frame
2. Founder goals and constraints
3. Customer and problem
4. Offer and monetization

8. Financial reality and risk

## 1) Planning frame

- What horizon are we planning for: `3`, `5`, or `10` years?
- What business are we planning: core product, portfolio, or parent company?
- What must this plan optimize for first: growth, profitability, cash safety, or optionality?

## 2) Founder goals and constraints

- What personal and business outcomes are non-negotiable?
- How much time/capital/risk are you willing to deploy in the next 12 months?
- Which constraints are fixed (location, team size, legal, ethics, lifestyle)?

## 3) Customer and problem

- Who is the highest-value customer segment?
- What critical problem are they paying to solve right now?
- What evidence do we have that this problem is urgent and expensive?

## 4) Offer and monetization

- What is the initial offer ladder (entry, core, premium)?
- What is the pricing logic and why should this customer accept it?
- Which revenue streams are in-scope (services, product, recurring, licensing, ads, affiliate)?

## 5) Market and competition

- What category are we really competing in?
- Which substitutes already solve this problem?
- What defensibility can we realistically build in 12-36 months?

## 6) Growth and channel strategy

- Which channels can produce repeatable customer acquisition in the next 2 quarters?
- What is the acquisition-retention-expansion loop?
- Which channel assumptions are untested and highest risk?

## 7) Operations and capability design

- What capabilities are required to win (product, sales, ops, compliance, finance)?
- Which capabilities are missing in-house today?
- What should be built internally vs outsourced vs automated?

## 8) Financial reality and risk

- What are current and target unit economics (CAC, gross margin, payback, churn)?
- What burn/runway or cash limits constrain the plan?
- What could kill the plan in the next 12 months and how do we detect it early?

## Prioritization rule

Select next questions using this order:

1. Decision-critical unknowns
2. High-risk assumptions
3. High-cost mistakes if wrong
4. Fastest path to reduce uncertainty
