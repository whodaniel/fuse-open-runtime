The personal cloud

Zo is a new kind of computer. Created by a new kind of computer company.

Our computer is cloud-native. Our goal is to give everyone the full power of the cloud. Previously, the great powers of cloud computing were only accessible to developers. The next billion developers are coming – and they need a cloud that's much simpler.

Our computer is also AI-native. Our goal is to make using Zo feel as powerful as having an entire software company at your command.

Who we are

Co-founders Rob & Ben met in 2013 on the early Venmo team.

Rob went on to become the founding engineer at Substack. Ben spent nearly a decade at Stripe.

They started a company together in 2023. First they built Substrate, an agentic inference platform for developers. Over time, Substrate evolved into something bigger: a personal AI cloud computer that lets anyone build with AI. They called it Zo.

How we work

We're a small team based in Brooklyn, NY. These are our core values:

Communicate with clarity and care.

We're operating heavy machinery. Real people depend on Zo for real work. We communicate with each other, and with our users, with the same precision and care we put into our software.

Our users are builders.

Though our users are "consumers," we see them as the next generation of developers. The art of abstraction design extends beyond code to concepts and interfaces. We're building a platform for this generation of builders, and we're obsessed with abstractions that last.

Thoughtful AI.

Automation is leverage. AI should be used as a tool for words, code, and work. We live on the bleeding edge of doing things with AI. But we also know that careless use creates fatigue, erodes trust, and carries real risk. We use it deliberately.

A love for the craft.

Even as software industrializes, we stay obsessed with the art and science of building it. Software is a stack of abstractions designed for humans and machines. Machines don't care about elegance. So we have to.

Try Zo

Zo is free to start. Get your own personal AI cloud computer at zo.computer/signup. See what Zo can do, or read the docs.
