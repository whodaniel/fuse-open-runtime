Clawdbot Video Frames (ffmpeg) Extract a single frame from a video, or create quick thumbnails for inspection. Quick start First frame: {baseDir}/scripts/frame.sh /path/to/video.mp4 --out /tmp/frame.jpg Copy At a timestamp: {baseDir}/scripts/frame.sh /path/to/video.mp4 --time 00:00:10 --out /tmp/frame-10s.jpg Copy Notes Prefer --time for “what is happening around here?”.
